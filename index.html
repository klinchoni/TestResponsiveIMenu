WHAT WE OFFER

We are a friendly team offering unforgettable holidays in Bansko. Our team includes experts in various sports.

We also have a professional team to guide you in skiing at Todorka Peak, one of the most impressive and beautiful granite peaks in Pirin, standing at 2,756 meters high.

  • Hiking and Trekking: Explore the majestic Pirin mountains with our experienced guides.
  • Rock Climbing: Learn rock climbing techniques from our skilled instructors.
  • Mountain Biking: Ride through scenic trails with our expert bikers.

Our Motivation

We believe in the power of nature to heal and inspire.

Our mission is to provide you with memorable adventures that rejuvenate your spirit and connect you with the beauty of Bansko.

  • In every conditions

    The weather can never stop our team to do what they love! So, if you, your family or your friends also love challenges and prefer nature then beach, do not hesitate to connect with us whenever you want to become part of our challenges.

  • Professional Team

    Our professional team will always help you with your difficulties or even they can provide you with medical assistance, if necessary.

  • Expert hikers

    Our team of expert hikers will teach you how to climb, how to overcome difficulties and what to beware of during the tour.
