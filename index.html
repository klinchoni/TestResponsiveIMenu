WHAT WE OFFER

We are a very friendly team.

We offer you an unforgetable holiday in Bansko. In our team, we have people for each sport you want. So, we have a team for skiing, if you can not ski, it is no problem, the teachers will teach you. After that you will be in the ski team and you will have fun in the mountain while skiing. Also, we have a professional team with who you can go skiing in Todorka peak, which is one of the most impressive and beautiful granite peaks in Pirin. It is 2,756 meters high and is located on the Todorino side ridge, which is a short ridge with three peaks - Big, Small and Middle Todorka. And at least but not last, we have a team of expert hikers. There in the spring or summer you can go with them and conquer all the beautiful mountain peaks
